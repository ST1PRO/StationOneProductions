About Us

ST1PRO Computer Services has been serving the needs of businesses, both large and small, for over a decade. Adam Olson began building and repairing computer systems for friends and family, out of their garage in the suburbs of Tempe AZ. The computer expert was receiving so many requests for computer Services that he decided to open ST1PRO with the vision of protecting and supporting Arizona's computer users. Soon, word of their outstanding service spread and the two could hardly keep up with demand. They knew that to be truly helpful, they would need to be able to answer the call for help 24 hours a day. Within a year, the staff at StationOne Productions Computer Services grew in number from 1 to 3.  ST1PRO Computer Services has now grown to a staff of nearly 5 professionals committed to delivering unparalleled service.

ST1PRO Computer Services is Arizona's most trusted name in Computer support, maintenance, and repair.
